BDD: BDD mean behaviour driven development.
TDD: Test driven development. 

For BDD there is a frameworks: 
1. Cucumber framework
2. Serinity framework.

In cucumber BA people will develop the feature files,
and share to the development people and QA people.
based on these feature files dvelopment people will develop the application,
and QA people will test the application.

In cucumber features files will develop using the below cucumber annotaions:
@Given
@When
@Then
@And
@But
in this test data will create in the feature files only.
